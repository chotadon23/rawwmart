How it works
Simple. Fast. Local.

From searching to doorstep delivery — the entire flow happens in minutes.

🛒
STEP 01

Vendor searches

Street vendors search for the raw materials they need, just like an online shopping app.

📍
STEP 02

Nearby suppliers appear

Suppliers within 5-10 km are recommended automatically based on location and stock.

📦
STEP 03

Order is placed

The vendor places the order. A notification instantly reaches the supplier.

🚚
STEP 04

Supplier delivers

The supplier accepts, prepares the order, and delivers it directly to the vendor's stall.
